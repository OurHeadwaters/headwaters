CODETRY
Build it yourself. Own it forever. Start from where you are.
One-liner:
Codetry is the method for people who see the problem clearly and are finally ready to build the solution — with tools they own, in a community they're already part of.

Overview:
Codetry is a digital self-reliance platform built for the people the startup world forgot — the ones who've been thinking in solutions for years but had no practical path to build. It uses literate programming and AI tools to give community leaders, homesteaders, and grassroots builders the method to create real, working systems without writing a line of traditional code. Not a bootcamp. Not a subscription. One-time access, lifetime ownership, built from the soil up.

The Person:
You've been carrying the vision for years — maybe decades. You juggle too many systems that don't talk to each other, squeeze through grindstones you didn't build, and know in your gut there's a better way to run a local economy. You're not looking for a startup. You're looking for a method. You want to build something that serves your community, stays in your hands, and doesn't require you to mold yourself to someone else's platform ever again. You're the soil. Codetry is the foundation.

The Core Promise:
After Codetry, you can build. Not hypothetically — actually. A working system, a community tool, a local economic engine. Yours. No ongoing fees, no dependency on tech bros, no waiting for someone else to hand you a solution.

The First Three Blueprints:

Blueprint 1 — Zone 0: Root Work
Health is infrastructure. Before you build anything else, you build the body and habits that make everything else possible. This blueprint maps what a stable Zone 0 actually looks like — and gives you the tools to get there and stay there.

Blueprint 2 — Zone 1: Follow the Money
Channel your income and watch the ripple effect. This blueprint teaches you to see where your dollars are going, redirect them with intention, and start building a financial picture that belongs to your household — not a bank's spreadsheet.

Blueprint 3 — Zone 3: Community Architecture
Build community like a pro, even if you'd rather be shovelling. The relational and organizational infrastructure of a local economy — how to run a table, hold a room, and create the conditions where people actually show up and stay.

The Bigger Picture:
Codetry isn't just a course. It's a node in a decentralized, grassroots economic system that's been building from the roots for fifteen years. The monocrop field is real. So is the fix. We are the soil — the activity network — and Codetry is how we start growing something different before the flood.
