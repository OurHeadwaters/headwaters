Headwaters Commerce — Context Pack for Business Review
Date: June 6, 2026
Purpose: Full picture of what was built in the payment/commerce layer. Written for the kitchen table — what exists, how it works, what a buyer experiences, and what questions remain open.

The Short Version
Headwaters now has a working storefront. Buyers can find products, pay through Stripe, receive their purchase by email, and access it from any device. The email list (Kit.com) captures every buyer. Everything is live and running.

What Is Being Sold
There are three distinct product categories:

1. Digital Kits (Parr's Jars)
PDF downloads. Instant delivery.

Product	Price	What it is

Buyer experience: Pay → receive email with download link → land on a thank-you page with a live download button. 3. The Goodbye Kit — Practitioner Application (stomping-path-documentation.replit.app/goodbye/practitioners-apply)
Entry point for practitioners who want a listed profile and badge.

How Payment Works (Under the Hood, Simply)
All payments go through Stripe — the industry standard, no Headwaters money is ever touched directly.
When a buyer pays, Stripe fires a webhook (an instant signal) to the Headwaters server, which records the purchase and sends the delivery email. This happens even if the buyer closes their browser before the thank-you page loads.
Each buyer gets a session link that is permanent — they can bookmark the thank-you page and re-download any time.
Second-device recovery: If a buyer paid on one device and wants access on another, they enter the email from their Stripe receipt. The system finds the purchase and re-issues access.
Email list: Every buyer is automatically added to Bobbie's Kit.com email list at the moment of purchase. The Kit.com integration is live as of today (credentials added June 6, 2026).
The Delivery Email
Every buyer receives an email after purchase. It contains:

A direct download link (for kit buyers)
A link to their permanent session/thank-you page
A "next step" pointer (pointing to the next relevant kit in the sequence)
For Course 1 buyers, the email confirms enrollment and points to the course.

Operational Safeguards Built In
Stripe link health check: A script runs before every deploy verifying all four payment links are live and resolving correctly. If a link is broken, it catches it before buyers see it.
PDF link check: A separate script verifies that all PDF download URLs are still resolving. Also runs automatically alongside the link check.
Smoke test passed: A full end-to-end purchase was verified (Task #847) — real payment, real access grant, real delivery. It works.
