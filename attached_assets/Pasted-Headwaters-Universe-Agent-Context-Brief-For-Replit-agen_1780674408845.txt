THE HEADWATERS UNIVERSE — WHAT IT IS
Headwaters is a network of self-reliance tools for northern communities, families, practitioners, and co-ops. Every tool belongs to one of six divisions. The tools are designed from the inside — Bobbie has lived inside every system she is building for.

The six divisions:

#	Division	Core idea
0	Headwaters Care	Tools for people caring for others — frictionless, person-centred, built from lived experience
1	Headwaters Finance	Income harnessing — buckets, reserves, four-bucket architecture, from households to municipalities
2	Headwaters Business	Income generating — business operating system, multi-reservoir practitioners
3	Headwaters Community	Collective structures — co-ops, non-profits, band councils, circular economy
4	Headwaters Media	Free knowledge commons — Stomping Grounds, Odyssey, Research Library, curation layer
5	Headwaters Studio	Creative tech — XRPL Studio, builder tools
The principle: All knowledge free. Curation converts. Every tool is better alone and transformative in the network.
