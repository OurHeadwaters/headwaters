The concept
Permaculture zones describe distance from the centre of activity (yourself) by how often you visit and how much energy you invest. The 807 Garden app maps the entire platform onto this model:

Zone 0 — You. Your identity, your membership tier, your private state
Zone 1–2 — Your Three Sisters. Three personal things you tend daily. Already built: named by the member (habits, connections, joys), private, checked off each day
Zone 3 — The co-op platform artifacts. Community infrastructure. Visited when needed, not daily. This is what's missing
Zone 4 — The wider membership ecosystem. Already built: the Community tab showing tiers as ecosystem layers
Zone 5 — The world beyond. Unbuilt, intentionally sparse
