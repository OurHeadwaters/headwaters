Revenue Streams & Projections
1. Workshop Board (Already Built)
The surplus-share model is smart. Hosts break even first, then the platform takes ~10% of proceeds beyond that.

Scale	Monthly Workshops	Avg Tickets	Avg Price	Platform Take
Early	20	15	$47	~$1,400/mo
Growth	75	25	$67	~$12,600/mo
Mature	200	35	$97	~$68,000/mo
The model self-selects quality hosts — they only pay when they're profitable.

2. Transformation Path Cohorts
Each of the 6 paths is a natural 8–12 week structured program with expert council involvement. This is the highest-leverage play.

Price: $297–$997/cohort
Target: 50–200 students per cohort launch
Revenue per launch: $15k–$200k
Cadence: 2–3 cohorts/year per path = potentially 12–18 launches/year across all paths
This is the flagship monetization — the paths are the curriculum.

3. Community Membership ("Brigade")
Gate progress syncing, cross-device history, the Stomping Grounds community features, and expert access behind a membership tier.

Price: $9–$19/month or $97/year
Target: 1–5% of TSP's existing audience
TSP has ~100k+ listeners → 1,000–5,000 members = $97k–$485k ARR
4. Value for Value (Already Built)
Voluntary Lightning tips. Hard to project, but TSP's audience skews toward people who believe in it ideologically. A passionate 1,000-listener V4V base at $5/month average = $5k/month.

5. Expert Council Directory / Consulting Access
Expert Council members pay for placement and inbound leads from path-aligned users.

Price: $99–$299/month per expert listing
20–50 experts = $2k–$15k/month recurring
6. Wishing Well Fulfillment
Users pay to request content or community help. Crowdfunded content requests, essentially. Small per-transaction take (5–10%). More brand-building than revenue early on.
