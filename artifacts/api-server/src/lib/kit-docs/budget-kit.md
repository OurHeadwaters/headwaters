# Budget Kit — Envelope Budgeting with Crypto Buckets

## What This Kit Does

The Budget Kit is a simple, powerful envelope budgeting system that works with both fiat and crypto (XRP, RLUSD, Bitcoin). It is designed for personal finances or small business, and it includes integration with the X-Buckets tool for non-custodial stablecoin budgeting.

This is not a complicated financial system. It is a system that actually works because it is honest about human psychology — we spend what we see, and we save what we protect.

## Core Pillars

### 1. The Envelope Framework
Every dollar gets a job before you spend it. You divide your income into categories — buckets — and spend only what is in each bucket. When a bucket is empty, you stop spending in that category. Simple. Honest. Effective.

### 2. Fiat Buckets
Your regular fiat income runs through the standard envelope system: housing, food, transportation, utilities, insurance, savings, giving, and discretionary. The exact categories are less important than the habit of pre-allocating everything.

### 3. Crypto Buckets
The X-Buckets tool extends the envelope system to non-custodial crypto. You hold your own keys. You set allocation rules. You can denominate buckets in XRP, RLUSD, or Bitcoin, and the system tracks your allocations across wallets without custodying your assets.

### 4. Debt Elimination Protocol
Before you can build, you need to clear the deck. The debt elimination protocol inside this kit is the same framework Jack has been teaching on TSP for 15+ years — it works, it is not complicated, and it does not require a financial advisor.

### 5. Small Business Application
The same envelope framework works for small businesses and solopreneurs. Operating expenses, tax reserves, owner's draw, reinvestment, and emergency reserves all get buckets. Never be surprised by a tax bill again.

## Starting Point

The envelope budgeting framework applies immediately to your current income. Start splitting your income into buckets this month. Do not wait for the perfect system — start with five buckets and refine from there.

Listen to the Mind & Money track's early episodes on financial philosophy, then set up your first budget buckets using the X-Buckets tool.

## What Done Looks Like

- All income pre-allocated into named buckets before spending begins
- High-interest debt on a documented payoff timeline
- 1-month cash reserve (3-month goal)
- At least one crypto bucket set up and funded with a small test amount
- Tax reserves handled automatically — no surprises at year-end
