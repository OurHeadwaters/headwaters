# Practitioner Kit — Tools for Movement Builders

## What This Kit Does

The Practitioner Kit is built for coaches, facilitators, permaculture designers, and movement builders who are serving others inside the self-reliance and sovereignty movement.

It contains the tools, frameworks, and professional systems you need to serve clients effectively while staying sovereign yourself. This is a consultative kit — access is granted by application and matched to your specific work.

## Who This Is For

- Certified permaculture designers serving residential and community clients
- Life and resilience coaches working with families and individuals
- Facilitators running workshops on preparedness, financial literacy, or homesteading
- Community organizers building local resilience networks
- Natural health practitioners integrating sovereign health practices

## Core Pillars

### 1. Client Intake and Assessment
The Headwaters intake tool is designed specifically for practitioners. It provides a structured intake process that surfaces a client's current zone, risk profile, and readiness for each transformation path. You can push results directly to your client file.

### 2. Curriculum and Sequencing
How you sequence content for a client matters enormously. This kit provides sequencing guides for each major transformation path, so you can build a personalized roadmap for each person you serve.

### 3. Group Facilitation
Running a group through a transformation is different from coaching an individual. This kit includes facilitation guides, discussion questions, and pacing recommendations for group cohort work.

### 4. Staying Sovereign as a Practitioner
The biggest trap for practitioners is building a practice that replicates the employee relationship — one big client, one platform, one certification body that can revoke your standing. This kit includes frameworks for building a diversified, sovereign practice.

### 5. Community Connection
Practitioners in this network are connected to each other through the Expert Council. You will have access to peer consultation, referral networks, and ongoing professional development.

## Access

The Practitioner Kit is consultative — not a self-serve purchase. Apply through the Headwaters Practitioner App. Access is granted based on your background, current work, and fit with the movement's values.

## What Done Looks Like

- Headwaters intake tool integrated into your client onboarding
- At least three clients progressing through a mapped transformation sequence
- Practice structured with multiple income streams and no single point of failure
- Connected to the practitioner peer network
- Contributing to the Expert Council knowledge base
